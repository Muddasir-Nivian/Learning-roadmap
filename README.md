# Orrbit — Learning Roadmap Tracker

> Consistency becomes easier when progress starts getting saved and tracked.

---

## Why I Built This

I was about to turn my soft skills — pattern recognition, complex 
analysis, curiosity of learning the backside mechanisms of running 
systems, systematic thinking, predictive analysis, and problem solving 
— into practical skills that serve real value.

To do that seriously I built a detailed learning roadmap. Not just 
covering tools and languages but keeping a structure that builds 
practical problem solving skills at its core.

As AI is evolving it is also taking over mechanical repetitive work 
— so the roadmap also covers the skill to audit and validate 
AI driven outputs. That way a person can invest their energy toward 
what needs pure human intellect.

When it came to building the tracker I applied the same thinking. 
AI has taken its place in web development and designing too — so I 
used it deliberately. I scratched the logic, features, theme, and 
functions myself and fed it to AI to execute.

---

## What It Does

- Tracks progress across all 18 months and 72 weeks
- Every month expands to show individual weeks
- Every week expands to show specific tasks
- Tasks are checkable and save automatically
- Circular progress indicators show completion per month
- Overall progress bar tracks the full journey
- No installation needed — runs in any browser
- Works offline — progress saves to local device storage

---

## How To Use It

1. Download the HTML file
2. Open it in any browser on any device
3. Check off tasks as you complete them
4. Progress saves automatically to your device

---

- Single file — no dependencies, no frameworks, no installation
